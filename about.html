Mission

We ought to provide high-quality mix and match products at affordable prices, making personalized style that is accessible for everyone — especially the students of CSN. We also aim to offer a diverse range of mix and match products that cater to various tastes, preferences, and occasions.

Vision

Our vision is to be a sustainable and ethical brand that offers innovative mix and match products, contributing to a positive impact on our community and the environment.

We thought about our food products depending on the needs of the students, and we knew they would be popular — we didn't just think based on what we wanted or what was trending. At first we thought it would be the one that would really appeal to the eyes and taste buds of the students and also those who would pass by our place.


We think of the one that people would eat in the morning because it is usually not heavy food, so that's why we immediately entered it. Our inspiration is Mix and Match. The only difference is that it's a sandwich, and you can pair whatever you want. That's why we came up with those because we make sure that our foods will be popular with every single person.


So, what are you waiting for? Come and try our food choices, which is not only appealing to look at but also good to taste! 🍱

Our goal is to earn money and also to reach the standard of taste of people who will eat from us. As students, we want to have experiences — especially those who plan to build a business in the future. We also want to know what to do as business owners.

Students

Especially the students of CSN who are looking for affordable, delicious, and filling meals during school days.
